# My Car Companion — Site Vision

## 1. Purpose
A marketing + product website that introduces My Car Companion to car owners and mechanics,
drives free account signups, and hosts required legal pages.

## 2. Audience
- **Primary**: Independent mechanics and shops looking to manage jobs, track vehicles, and connect with clients
- **Secondary**: Car owners who want to track maintenance, find mechanics, and manage their vehicle history
